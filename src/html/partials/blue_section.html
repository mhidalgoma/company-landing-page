3 Proven benefits

Weight lost

Because olive oil helps keep blood sugar levels stable, it can help curb cravings that might otherwise lead to overeating and weight gain.

Healthy immune system

Eating 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil every day is associated with greater growth of immune cells that attack foreign invaders.

It combats pain

Extra virgin olive oil contains the polyphenol oleocanthal, which has demonstrated anti-inflammatory properties similar to ibuprofen.
